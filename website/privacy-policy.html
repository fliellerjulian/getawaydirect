Introduction

Thank you for choosing getaway.direct. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use the getaway.direct Chrome Extension and related services (collectively, "Services").

1. Information We Collect

getaway.direct is designed to operate with minimal data collection. Here’s what we collect:

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymized usage data to improve the Services. This includes information such as the number of times the extension is used, error reports, and general performance data.
    ‍
  • Publicly Available Data: When you browse Airbnb or other platforms with the extension active, we only access publicly available information to provide direct booking links or other data relevant to the service.
    ‍
  • No Personal Data: getaway.direct does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, payment details, or contact information. All actions performed by the extension remain on your local device.

2. How We Use the Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Improve and Maintain the Service: Usage data and performance metrics help us understand how users interact with the extension, troubleshoot issues, and enhance functionality.
    ‍
  • Ensure Security: Anonymized data allows us to monitor for abnormal or suspicious activity and protect the integrity of the service.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or rent any collected information with third parties. However, getaway.direct may rely on third-party service providers (such as SerpApi for search functionalities) to process data for the sole purpose of delivering core features. These providers are obligated to comply with privacy and security requirements, ensuring that your data is handled responsibly.

4. Security Measures

We take reasonable security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and while we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Choices

  • Extension Deactivation: You may disable or uninstall the getaway.direct Chrome Extension at any time via your browser’s settings.
    ‍
  • Privacy Preferences: Because the extension does not collect personal information, no opt-out or additional privacy settings are required.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in the service or legal requirements. All updates will be posted within the extension’s official documentation or our website, with a revised date indicated at the top.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@getaway.direct
